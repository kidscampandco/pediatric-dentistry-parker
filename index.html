Frequently Asked Questions

What age should my child start preventive dental care?

By age one or within six months of the first tooth. Early visits help monitor development and build comfort.

How often should my child see a dentist?

Every six months, or as recommended by your pediatric dentist in Parker based on their needs.

Are fluoride treatments safe for children?

Yes, fluoride is a safe and effective way to protect teeth from cavities when applied by professionals.

What do sealants do?

Sealants are protective layers applied to molars to prevent decay in deep grooves and pits.

How can I help my child practice good oral hygiene at home?

Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste, floss, reduce sugary snacks, and make dental care a positive habit.
